About me

Thanks for being here. Atticus (my cat) and I are really pleased to welcome you.

Over the years I have dabbled with various forms of art. I have always enjoyed creating but lacked the confidence to pursue it. During the pandemic I started drawing again. I have anxiety and depression and I found it really mindful to focus on a drawing.

I started taking requests for drawings as a bit of fun. It was super rewarding to draw something for someone. This led to pet commissions and designing products.

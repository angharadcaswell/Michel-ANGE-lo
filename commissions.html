Commission process

Step 1

Email over your idea and photos. Don't worry! If this feels overwhelming, we can talk it through. You do not need to come up with the design just give me some pointers so we can make it personal. Please send me a selection of photos to give me the best chance of capturing your lovely pet.

Step 2

1-1 call with Ange to confirm design idea. We can decide on background and colour themes. Once agreed I will send you the payment details. Full payment is required.

Step 3

Sign off on your drawing. A preview photo will be sent to you, although please note that the artist holds the final decision regarding design rights.

Step 4

Your commission will be posted to you. Enjoy! Please allow up to 4 weeks for the commission to be ready and with you.
